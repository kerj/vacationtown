Hakone Summer Matsuri Festivals

July 31st-August 5th

"The story of a dragon that lived in Lake Ashi, who would inflict damage on the lives of people living near the Lake. A chief priest of the shrine used his magical powers to change the dragon into a 9-headed protective spirit."

Local Legend

Order of festivities

  • July 31st- Ashonoko Kosui Matsuri
  • August 1st- Hakone Shrine Reidai Festival
  • August 2nd- Gojinkousai
  • August 4th- Kojiri Dragon Festival
  • August 5th- Festival of the Burning Shrine Gates
